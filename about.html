Let's Get Those Hands Dirty

A researcher turned UX designer, at the beginning of her creative journey.
I am passionate about the research and visual processes of creating web-based solutions, currently acquainting myself with the technical elements as well.


My aim is to acquire a well-rounded set of skills which will allow to help create simple, valuable and meaningful solutions.


When it is playtime, I am focused on getting some motion and fresh air with moderate amounts of running, swimming and yoga or organic farming. Highly appreciative of fireplaces, a cup of hot beverage, a cozy knitting project or a good book.

Work Process

In my approach to projects, I rely on critical thinking and holistic problem solving. Trained in research and fine arts, together we can ensure that the solutions we create are backed up by data and designed with an aesthetic eye, while keeping the big picture view of our users, businesses and sustainability.

Collaboration is a way to get as many brains as possible onto a project to improve the end-product, while also ensuring coming away with something new, we learned.


Constructive feedback is something I seek; and the best persuasive tool is a well formulated argument. During projects I prefer keeping a steady pace from the beginning, making sure that there is enough juice left for a semi-graceful finish.

Inquire

  • Understand the problem
  • Develop a hypothesis and research questions
  • Gather standard data
  • Listen actively to users
  • Observe, define personas, gain focus

Ideate

  • Organize and integrate research results to support ideation
  • Challenge assumptions
  • Brainstorms, affinity diagrams, card sorting
  • Sketching, low-fi wireframes
  • Develop IA

Prototype & Test

  • Develop high fidelity prototypes in XD
  • User testing in person or remotely
  • Iterate to refine solution
  • Communicate with developers
