about

A researcher turned UX designer, at the beginning of her creative journey.
I am passionate about the research and visual processes of creating a website and slowly acquainting myself with the technical elements as well.


In my approach to projects, I rely on critical thinking, holistic problem-solving, accommodating users’, stakeholders’ and the environment’s best interest.
My aim is to acquire a well-rounded set of skills which will allow to help create simple, valuable and meaningful solutions.


When it is playtime, I am focused on getting some motion and fresh air with moderate amounts of running, swimming and yoga or organic farming. Highly appreciative of fireplaces, a cup of hot beverage, a cozy knitting project or a good book.


The Process

Inquire

  • Understand the problem

  • Develop a hypothesis and research questions

  • Gather standard data

  • Listen actively to users

  • Observe, define personas, gain focus

Ideate

  • Organize and integrate research results to support ideation

  • Challenge assumptions

  • Brainstorms, affinity diagrams, card sorting

  • Sketching, low-fi wireframes

  • Develop IA

Prototype & Test

  • Develop high fidelity prototypes in XD

  • User testing in person or remotely

  • Iterate to refine solution

  • Communicate with developers
